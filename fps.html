    COD: Warzone

    Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale and extraction shooter developed by Infinity Ward and Raven Software, and published by Activision. Set in the Call of Duty universe, it features fast-paced gunplay, realistic military combat, and expansive maps like Verdansk and Al Mazrah. Players can squad up or go solo, looting, surviving, and battling to be the last one standing. Warzone also includes unique mechanics like the Gulag and in-game contracts, setting it apart from other games in the genre.
