The Sims 4

Developed by Maxis and published by Electronic Arts, The Sims 4 lets players create and control people, known as "Sims," managing their lives, relationships, careers, and homes. With its sandbox style and countless expansion packs, it offers deep customization and storytelling, making it one of the most iconic simulation franchises in gaming.
