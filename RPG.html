Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play action RPG developed by HoYoverse (formerly miHoYo), featuring a vast open world filled with elemental magic, exploration, and fast-paced combat. Set in the fantasy realm of Teyvat, players control a roster of characters with unique elemental abilities, switching between them in real-time to solve puzzles and defeat enemies. Its gacha system and regular updates make it a global hit.
