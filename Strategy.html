Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans, by Supercell, is a mobile strategy game where players build and upgrade a village, train troops, and battle other players to earn resources. Known for its clan-based warfare and real-time strategy elements, it combines base defense with competitive multiplayer in a highly addictive format.
