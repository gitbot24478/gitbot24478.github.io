Palworld

Palworld is a unique mix of monster-catching, crafting, and survival mechanics developed by Pocketpair. Often described as “Pokémon with guns,” players collect and battle creatures called “Pals” while also engaging in farming, building, and combat. The game blends cute visuals with surprisingly dark and complex survival elements.
